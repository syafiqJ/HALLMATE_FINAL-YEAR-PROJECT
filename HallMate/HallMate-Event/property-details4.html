Beach Scenery, Photography Package, Catering Services

Description

Rumah Abang Jamil Pantai Balok, located near Balok Beach, Kuantan, is a charming event venue designed with a classic Malay village concept, inspired by the traditional architecture of the 1980s. Featuring an open-air spaces, it offers a unique and rustic setting ideal for weddings, ceremonies, receptions, and private celebrations. The venue comfortably accommodates medium to large events and provides complete packages that include wedding decorations, catering, and spacious outdoor areas. Making it a popular choice for couples seeking a romantic, nature-inspired event space.
