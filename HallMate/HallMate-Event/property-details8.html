Description

Timorra Venue Kuantan is a grand, full-service event space located at Tanjung Lumpur, Kuantan, featuring a sweeping 10,000 ft² fully air‑conditioned hall accompanied by a VIP suite, bridal preparation room, prayer facilities, and 120 on-site parking bays. Designed for weddings and corporate events, it accommodates between 300 to 1,000 guests, offering tailored Classic and Exclusive packages that include stage, seating, buffet stations, décor, photography, basic lighting, PA system, and even videography.
